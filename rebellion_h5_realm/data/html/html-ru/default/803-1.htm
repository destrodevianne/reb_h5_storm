A detailed description of the GvG tournament
1. Both groups are taking part in the tournament must complete a simple task: collect a greater number of points than the opponent team in the specified time on the screen and destroy the *Guardian Treasure Herald*. 2. At the beginning of the tournament teams are near their flag. Around the flag is set to collect a peace zone, rebafa. It is absolutely safe, but not great. 3. In each room there are dungeons Mutant Treasure Chest with a lot of health, the destruction of each of which will bring the team 20 points. In total there are 9 chests. 4. 10 minutes after the start of the tournament appears *Guardian Treasure Herald*, as well as opening the door to the treasury. Killing this boss will bring the team 150 points and *finished the tournament*. 5. If neither team has failed to destroy the boss in the allotted time, the winner will be determined at the expiration of a timer event on the screen. 6. Each kill an opposing player will your team 5 points 7. Every death from the hand of an opposing player takes away from your team 3 points 8. Killer boss chest and the player is determined by the deposition of the last strike. 9. If the player either team is killed by the hands of a monster or another player, he immediately resurrected near his flag, his HP, CP, MP restored. 10. If a player of any team will try to get out of the group - he will be disqualified and thrown out of the dungeon 11. If a player of any team will try to teleport out of the dungeon - he will be disqualified and thrown out of the dungeon 12. If a player of any team will try to get out of the game, or break the association will not have time to reconnect rezhie NO CARRIER - he will be disqualified and thrown out of the dungeon 13. If any group of teams will be disbanded, its opponent will pay 200 points, tournament will end immediately and all participants of the collapsed group will be disqualified 14. The counter on the screen displays the current team's score. For more information, teams can be viewed by clicking on the icon crossed swords in the upper right corner near the radar. 15. One minute before the end of the tournament system message will inform both teams about it. 16. Each participant of the tournament has its own personal account. Killing the enemy team player gives a personal point, death does not diminish the personal account. 17. Dungeon is the type of PvP, with the death experience is not lost, PvP counter is not increased, there are rules in PvP combat zone. 18. Teams not marked externally, each player must know his or her party members. To attack enemy players can not forceattack, its possible to attack only through forceattack. Penalty for the murder of his party members not present, this does not affect the killing by both teams.

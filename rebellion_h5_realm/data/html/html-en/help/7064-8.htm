Diary of Lydia:
Dark Deeps

Starlight reflected in his smile.
He speaks of courage, but I do not know where or even who I am!
Can crazy people can be courageous? Who knows?
His laughter is heard, and again, the word courage. Other words, but they are in another language, I do not understand.
Dark Magician with his smile speaks softly to me, holding my hands in the dark night.
Courage. Courage. Courage. Nothing else matters.
My ceremony is still ready! What's going on here so late?
